Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Travel Buddy Asia is an AI-powered travel planning platform designed specifically for Malaysian travelers. We help you create detailed, personalized itineraries with accurate budget breakdowns and cultural considerations.

We offer both free and premium plans. The free Explorer plan allows you to create up to 3 itineraries per month for trips up to 3 days. For more features and longer trips, check out our Premium (Gold Wanderer) plan.

Account & Subscription

You can easily create an account using your Google account. Simply click the "Continue with Google" button on our homepage and follow the prompts.

To upgrade your subscription, visit the Pricing page from the navigation menu and select your preferred plan. You can also upgrade through your Profile page.

Itinerary Planning

Our budget estimates are based on real-time data and local market rates. We provide detailed breakdowns including transportation, accommodation, food, and activities, with both budget and premium options.
