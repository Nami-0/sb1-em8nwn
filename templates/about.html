About Travel Buddy Asia

Our Story

Travel Buddy Asia was born from a mission to revolutionize how Malaysian travelers plan and experience their travels, bringing transparency to travel costs and eliminating unnecessary markups.

As Malaysian travelers ourselves, we understand the challenges of planning affordable international trips. Through extensive research and feedback from the Malaysian travel community, we discovered a need for clearer, more transparent travel pricing. That's why we created a platform that puts honest pricing and cultural authenticity at the forefront of travel planning for Malaysians.

Our AI-powered platform breaks down the true costs of travel, from halal food options to local transportation, helping you understand exactly what you need to budget for your dream destination. No hidden fees, no per-person markups - just straightforward planning tools that respect your wallet and values.

Leadership

Nami (Shahrul Nazmi)

Founder

A passionate advocate for accessible travel and AI technology enthusiast, Nami founded Travel Buddy with a personal mission: to make international travel achievable for every Malaysian and the world. Drawing from his experience at a leading social media platform where he transitioned from marketing (generating over USD 500,000 in first-year revenue) to AI data operations, Nami brings a unique blend of business acumen and technological insight to Travel Buddy.

Having grown up with limited travel opportunities and only recently making his first international trip to Singapore, Nami intimately understands the challenges many Malaysians face when it comes to travel planning and budgeting. This personal experience, combined with his 4-year leadership background in customer service management, drives Travel Buddy's mission to democratize travel through transparent pricing and AI-powered planning tools. His vision is to ensure that no traveler's dream of exploring the world is limited by lack of information or planning resources.
